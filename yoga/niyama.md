### Sutra 2.32 
The *niyamas* are:
- *Śauca* = Cleansing, purifying
- *santoṣa* = contentment
- *tapaḥ* = austerity
- *svādhyāya* = study of scriptures, chanting of mantras
- *iśvara-praṇidhāna* = devotion to isvara, the supreme being or god
